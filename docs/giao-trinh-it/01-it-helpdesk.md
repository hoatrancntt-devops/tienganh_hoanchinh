# IT Helpdesk / IT Support — Tiếng Anh giao tiếp nghề nghiệp

Tài liệu này dành cho nhân viên **IT Helpdesk**, **Service Desk**, **IT Support** người Việt đang hoặc sắp làm việc với người dùng và đồng nghiệp nước ngoài.
Dùng khi bạn phải nhận ticket bằng tiếng Anh, hỗ trợ qua chat/điện thoại/remote, viết email thông báo sự cố, hoặc escalate lên team cấp cao hơn.
Trình độ mục tiêu: **B1–B2**. Bạn không cần tiếng Anh hoàn hảo — bạn cần tiếng Anh *rõ ràng, lịch sự và đúng nghiệp vụ*.

---

## 1. Từ vựng cốt lõi (Core Vocabulary)

| Term | Definition (EN) | Example Sentence | Tiếng Việt |
|---|---|---|---|
| **ticket** | A record of a user's issue or request in the helpdesk system | "I've logged a ticket for you — the number is INC0042318." | phiếu yêu cầu / phiếu sự cố |
| **incident** | An unplanned interruption or reduction in service quality | "We're treating this as a P1 incident since the whole floor is offline." | sự cố |
| **service request** | A user asking for something standard, not a failure | "A new mailbox is a service request, not an incident." | yêu cầu dịch vụ |
| **escalate** | To pass an issue to a higher support tier or manager | "If we can't fix it in 30 minutes, I'll escalate it to the network team." | chuyển lên cấp cao hơn |
| **SLA** (Service Level Agreement) | The agreed time limit to respond to or resolve an issue | "Our SLA for high-priority tickets is four business hours." | cam kết mức dịch vụ |
| **priority** | How urgent and impactful an issue is | "I'm raising the priority to High because it's blocking payroll." | mức độ ưu tiên |
| **root cause** | The underlying reason a problem happened | "The root cause was an expired certificate, not the browser." | nguyên nhân gốc |
| **workaround** | A temporary fix while the real fix is pending | "As a workaround, please use the web version until tomorrow." | giải pháp tạm |
| **remote session** | A session where support takes control of the user's screen | "May I start a remote session so I can see what you're seeing?" | phiên điều khiển từ xa |
| **credentials** | Username and password (or token) used to sign in | "Please never share your credentials, even with IT." | thông tin đăng nhập |
| **reset** (password) | To set a new password for a locked or forgotten account | "I've reset your password — you'll be asked to change it at first login." | đặt lại mật khẩu |
| **lockout** | An account temporarily blocked after failed login attempts | "Three bad attempts triggered an account lockout." | khoá tài khoản |
| **provision / deprovision** | To create or remove a user's accounts and access | "HR asked us to deprovision his accounts by Friday." | cấp / thu hồi tài khoản |
| **permissions** | What a user is allowed to do in a system | "You have read permissions but not write permissions on that folder." | quyền truy cập |
| **shared drive** | A network folder several users can access | "The file is on the shared drive under Finance/2026." | ổ đĩa dùng chung |
| **VPN** | A secure tunnel to the company network from outside | "Connect to the VPN first, then open the ERP." | mạng riêng ảo |
| **MFA / 2FA** | A second login step, usually a code or app approval | "Approve the MFA prompt on your phone within 30 seconds." | xác thực đa yếu tố |
| **print queue** | The list of jobs waiting at a printer | "Your job is stuck in the print queue — I'll clear it." | hàng đợi in |
| **driver** | Software that lets Windows talk to a device | "The printer driver is out of date on your laptop." | trình điều khiển |
| **spooler** | The Windows service that manages printing | "Restarting the print spooler usually fixes this." | dịch vụ quản lý in |
| **peripheral** | An external device: mouse, monitor, headset, dock | "Try plugging the peripheral into a different USB port." | thiết bị ngoại vi |
| **reboot / restart** | To turn the machine off and on again properly | "Please do a full reboot, not just close the lid." | khởi động lại |
| **replicate** | To reproduce the problem yourself to confirm it | "I couldn't replicate the error on my machine." | tái hiện lỗi |
| **downtime** | Time a service is unavailable | "Expected downtime is 20 minutes." | thời gian ngừng dịch vụ |
| **knowledge base (KB)** | The internal library of fix articles | "There's a KB article for this — I'll send you the link." | kho tri thức |
| **onboarding** | Setting up everything a new employee needs | "Her onboarding checklist includes laptop, email, and VPN." | quy trình tiếp nhận nhân sự mới |
| **asset tag** | The ID label used to track a device | "Can you read me the asset tag on the bottom of the laptop?" | mã tài sản |
| **triage** | Quickly sorting tickets by urgency | "I triage the queue every morning at 8." | phân loại ưu tiên |
| **follow up** | To check back on an open issue | "I'll follow up with you tomorrow morning." | theo dõi / liên hệ lại |
| **resolve / close** | To finish and formally end a ticket | "I'll close the ticket once you confirm it's working." | xử lý xong / đóng ticket |

> **Người Việt hay sai — phát âm:** `queue` đọc là /kjuː/ (giống chữ "Q"), **không** đọc "kiu-ê". `suite` /swiːt/ chứ không phải "suit". `Azure` /ˈæʒər/. `cache` đọc là /kæʃ/ giống "cash", không phải "ca-chê". `router` người Anh đọc /ˈruːtə/, người Mỹ /ˈraʊtər/ — cả hai đều được chấp nhận.

## 2. Mẫu câu giao tiếp (Common Phrases)

### Báo cáo (Reporting)

1. **Formal** — "I'm writing to confirm that the issue reported in ticket [INC0042318] has been resolved." *(Tôi xác nhận sự cố trong ticket đã được xử lý.)*
2. **Formal** — "Please find below a summary of the incident, the impact, and the corrective action taken." *(Dưới đây là tóm tắt sự cố, mức ảnh hưởng và biện pháp khắc phục.)*
3. **Informal** — "Quick update: mail is back up for everyone. Still watching it." *(Cập nhật nhanh: mail chạy lại rồi, vẫn đang theo dõi.)*
4. **Informal** — "Heads-up — three users on the 4th floor reported the same thing." *(Báo trước — 3 người tầng 4 báo cùng lỗi.)*

### Yêu cầu (Requesting)

5. **Formal** — "Could you please confirm your employee ID so I can verify the account?" *(Anh/chị cho tôi mã nhân viên để xác minh tài khoản?)*
6. **Formal** — "We would need your manager's written approval before granting this access." *(Chúng tôi cần phê duyệt bằng văn bản của quản lý trước khi cấp quyền này.)*
7. **Informal** — "Can you send me a screenshot of the full error message?" *(Gửi giúp mình ảnh chụp toàn bộ thông báo lỗi nhé?)*
8. **Informal** — "Mind if I take control of your screen for a minute?" *(Cho mình điều khiển màn hình một phút được không?)*

### Giải thích (Explaining)

9. **Formal** — "The interruption was caused by a scheduled certificate renewal that did not complete as expected." *(Gián đoạn do việc gia hạn chứng chỉ theo lịch không hoàn tất như dự kiến.)*
10. **Formal** — "To clarify, this is a limitation of the current licence, not a fault on your device." *(Xin nói rõ, đây là giới hạn của bản quyền hiện tại, không phải lỗi máy anh/chị.)*
11. **Informal** — "In plain terms: your password expired, that's all." *(Nói đơn giản: mật khẩu hết hạn, chỉ vậy thôi.)*
12. **Informal** — "Think of the VPN like a door key — without it the building won't let you in." *(Hình dung VPN như chìa khoá cửa — không có thì không vào được.)*

### Xử lý sự cố (Troubleshooting)

13. **Formal** — "May I ask a few questions to narrow down the cause?" *(Cho phép tôi hỏi vài câu để khoanh vùng nguyên nhân?)*
14. **Formal** — "Could you walk me through exactly what you did just before the error appeared?" *(Anh/chị mô tả giúp các bước ngay trước khi lỗi xuất hiện?)*
15. **Informal** — "Let's try the simple stuff first — restart and tell me if it's still there." *(Thử cách đơn giản trước — khởi động lại rồi báo mình.)*
16. **Informal** — "Does it happen every time, or only sometimes?" *(Lỗi xảy ra mọi lần hay chỉ thỉnh thoảng?)*
17. **Informal** — "Good news — I can reproduce it on my side, so it's not just you." *(Tin tốt — mình tái hiện được lỗi, nên không phải do máy anh.)*
18. **Formal** — "I'm afraid this is outside my team's scope; I'll escalate it to the application owner today." *(Rất tiếc việc này ngoài phạm vi của team tôi; hôm nay tôi sẽ chuyển lên chủ quản ứng dụng.)*

> **Lỗi lịch sự người Việt hay mắc:** đừng nói cộc lốc **"You must restart"** hay **"Do it now"** với khách/người dùng nước ngoài — nghe như ra lệnh. Dùng **"Could you please restart…"** hoặc **"Would you mind restarting…"**. Ngược lại, cũng đừng xin lỗi quá nhiều. Nếu lỗi *không* do bạn, nói **"I'm sorry you're dealing with this"** (đồng cảm) chứ không phải **"I'm so sorry, it's my fault"** (nhận lỗi sai). Nếu lỗi *đúng là do IT*, nói thẳng một lần: **"That was our mistake, and I've fixed it."** — người phương Tây đánh giá cao sự thẳng thắn hơn là xin lỗi lặp lại.

## 3. Tình huống thực tế (Real-world Scenarios)

### Kịch bản 1 — Điện thoại: người dùng không đăng nhập được
*Bối cảnh: Chị Anna gọi vào hotline lúc 8h30 sáng, giọng gấp vì sắp họp. Người dùng không rành kỹ thuật.*

- **Agent:** IT Service Desk, this is Minh speaking. How can I help you?
- **Anna:** I can't log in to my laptop. It keeps saying my password is wrong. I have a meeting in ten minutes.
- **Agent:** I understand — let's get you in quickly. Can I confirm your full name and employee ID, please?
- **Anna:** Anna Weber, ID 20415.
- **Agent:** Thank you. I can see three failed attempts, so the account is temporarily **locked out**. That's a security feature, nothing wrong with your laptop.
- **Anna:** Oh. So it's not broken?
- **Agent:** Not at all. I'm unlocking it now and resetting the password. Your temporary password is `Winter#2026`, all one word. You'll be asked to change it at first login.
- **Anna:** Got it. It's letting me in now — thank you so much.
- **Agent:** Great. I'll leave ticket INC0043117 open until this afternoon in case it repeats. Have a good meeting.

### Kịch bản 2 — Chat: máy in không in được
*Bối cảnh: Hỗ trợ qua Microsoft Teams. Người dùng đã thử vài lần và hơi bực.*

- **User:** printer on 3rd floor is dead again. 4th time this month.
- **Agent:** Sorry you're hitting this again — that's frustrating. Is it the HP by the pantry, asset tag PRN-0312?
- **User:** yes that one
- **Agent:** Thanks. Do you see an error on the printer screen, or does the job just disappear?
- **User:** job just sits there and says "printing" forever
- **Agent:** That sounds like a stuck **print queue**. I'll clear the queue and restart the **spooler** on the print server — you don't need to do anything.
- **User:** ok
- **Agent:** Done. Could you try one test page for me?
- **User:** it printed 🎉
- **Agent:** Perfect. This is the third repeat, so I'm raising a **problem ticket** to find the **root cause** instead of just clearing it each time. I'll update you next week.

### Kịch bản 3 — Walk-in: nhân viên mang laptop đến bàn IT
*Bối cảnh: Nhân viên sales đến trực tiếp quầy IT, laptop rất chậm.*

- **User:** Hi — my laptop has been really slow since yesterday. Can you look at it now?
- **Agent:** Sure, let's have a look. Just so I understand — slow at startup, slow in one app, or slow all the time?
- **User:** Mostly when I open the CRM. Everything freezes.
- **Agent:** Okay. Did anything change yesterday — an update, a new program, anything installed?
- **User:** There was a Windows update in the morning I think.
- **Agent:** That's a useful clue, thank you. Would you mind leaving the laptop with me for about 45 minutes? I'd like to check the logs properly rather than guess.
- **User:** I need it for a call at 11.
- **Agent:** Understood. Then let me do a quick check now and book a proper slot at 2 p.m. Does that work?
- **User:** Yes, that's fine.
- **Agent:** Great — I'll put it in as ticket REQ0088204 and see you at 2.

### Kịch bản 4 — Yêu cầu cấp quyền bị từ chối
*Bối cảnh: Người dùng xin quyền vào thư mục lương. IT phải từ chối lịch sự.*

- **User:** Can you give me access to the HR payroll folder? I need a file from it.
- **Agent:** I can help you get the file, but I'm not able to grant that access myself — payroll is restricted by policy.
- **User:** But I really need it today.
- **Agent:** I hear you. The fastest route is a short approval email from the HR data owner. With that, I can enable access within an hour.
- **User:** Who is the owner?
- **Agent:** That's Ms. Tran in HR Operations. Would you like me to send her the request and copy you in?
- **User:** Yes please, that would help.
- **Agent:** Done — I've raised REQ0088310 and asked her for approval. If it's only one file you need, she may simply email it to you, which is even quicker.
- **User:** Great, thanks for sorting it.

### Kịch bản 5 — Escalation nội bộ trong team
*Bối cảnh: Thảo luận nội bộ giữa L1 và L2 khi sự cố vượt khả năng xử lý.*

- **L1 (Nam):** Hi Priya, I need to escalate INC0043390. VPN failing for remote users in Hanoi.
- **L2 (Priya):** How many users, and since when?
- **L1:** Eleven reports since 07:40. All get "authentication failed", but their passwords work fine on the intranet.
- **L2:** So it's not credentials. Did you check the RADIUS logs?
- **L1:** I don't have access to those. That's why I'm passing it over.
- **L2:** Fair enough. Have you got a workaround in place?
- **L1:** Yes — I told them to use the web portal for email. It covers most of what they need this morning.
- **L2:** Good call. Set it to P2, add the eleven users to the ticket, and I'll take it from here.
- **L1:** Will do. I'll also post a banner on the intranet so we stop getting duplicate tickets.

### Kịch bản 6 — Lỗi do IT gây ra, phải xin lỗi đúng mức
*Bối cảnh: IT đẩy nhầm bản cập nhật khiến Outlook của một phòng ban không mở được.*

- **Agent:** Good morning Mr. Klein. I'm calling about the Outlook problem in your department.
- **Klein:** Yes, nobody in Finance can open email. It's chaos.
- **Agent:** I want to be straight with you: this was our mistake. We deployed an update overnight that wasn't compatible with your Office version.
- **Klein:** So how long?
- **Agent:** We started the rollback at 08:15. It's about 60% complete and we expect everyone back by 09:30.
- **Klein:** And in the meantime?
- **Agent:** Outlook on the web is working normally — same mailbox, same emails. I'll send the link to the whole department in two minutes.
- **Klein:** Alright. Please make sure this doesn't repeat.
- **Agent:** Understood. We're adding a pilot group to our deployment process so updates hit ten machines before they hit two hundred. I'll send you a short incident report by end of day.

### Kịch bản 7 — Onboarding nhân sự mới
*Bối cảnh: Trao đổi nội bộ giữa Helpdesk và HR trước ngày nhân viên mới đi làm.*

- **HR:** We have a new engineer starting Monday — David Ho. What do you need from me?
- **Agent:** Start date, job title, line manager, and which systems he needs beyond the standard set.
- **HR:** Monday the 27th, DevOps Engineer, reporting to Long. He'll need the code repositories and the staging servers.
- **Agent:** Noted. The standard package is laptop, AD account, mailbox, VPN and MFA — I can have that ready Friday.
- **HR:** And the repositories?
- **Agent:** Those need Long's approval since they're restricted. Could you ask him to reply to the onboarding ticket?
- **HR:** Sure. Anything else?
- **Agent:** One thing — please have him bring photo ID on day one so we can enrol MFA on his phone in person.
- **HR:** Will do. Thanks Minh.

## 4. Email mẫu (Email Templates)

### 4.1 Incident notification — Thông báo sự cố

```text
Subject: [P1 INCIDENT] [SERVICE_NAME] unavailable — investigation in progress

Dear all,

We are currently experiencing an unplanned outage affecting [SERVICE_NAME].

Start time:      [START_TIME] ([TIMEZONE])
Impact:          [WHO_IS_AFFECTED — e.g. all users in the Hanoi office]
Symptoms:        [SHORT_DESCRIPTION — e.g. users cannot sign in]
Current status:  Under investigation by [TEAM_NAME]
Workaround:      [WORKAROUND_OR "None available at this time"]
Reference:       [TICKET_ID]

Our next update will be sent by [NEXT_UPDATE_TIME], or sooner if the
service is restored. There is no need to raise additional tickets for
this issue.

We apologise for the disruption.

[YOUR_NAME]
IT Service Desk — [COMPANY_NAME]
```

### 4.2 Maintenance announcement — Thông báo bảo trì

```text
Subject: [PLANNED MAINTENANCE] [SYSTEM_NAME] — [DATE], [START_TIME]–[END_TIME]

Dear colleagues,

Please be informed of scheduled maintenance on [SYSTEM_NAME].

Window:          [DATE], [START_TIME] to [END_TIME] ([TIMEZONE])
Expected impact: [e.g. The system will be unavailable for up to 30 minutes]
Reason:          [REASON — e.g. security patching and disk expansion]
Action required: [e.g. Please save your work and sign out before START_TIME]

Services NOT affected: [LIST_OF_UNAFFECTED_SERVICES]

If the work finishes early, we will confirm by email. Should you notice
any issue after [END_TIME], please raise a ticket referencing [CHANGE_ID].

Thank you for your cooperation.

[YOUR_NAME]
IT Service Desk — [COMPANY_NAME]
```

### 4.3 Status report — Báo cáo tiến độ ticket

```text
Subject: [UPDATE] [TICKET_ID] — [SHORT_ISSUE_TITLE]

Hi [USER_NAME],

Here is an update on your ticket.

Reported:        [REPORTED_DATE]
Issue:           [SHORT_DESCRIPTION]
Progress so far: [WHAT_HAS_BEEN_DONE]
Current status:  [In progress / Pending vendor / Awaiting your input]
Next step:       [NEXT_ACTION] — owned by [OWNER_NAME]
Target:          [TARGET_DATE_TIME]

[If input is needed:]
To move forward, could you please [WHAT_YOU_NEED_FROM_THE_USER] by
[DEADLINE]? Without this, the ticket will remain on hold.

Thank you for your patience. Please reply to this email if anything
has changed on your side.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
IT Service Desk
```

### 4.4 Access request — Yêu cầu / phê duyệt cấp quyền

```text
Subject: [ACCESS REQUEST] [REQUESTER_NAME] — [SYSTEM_OR_FOLDER_NAME]

Dear [APPROVER_NAME],

We have received an access request that requires your approval as the
owner of [SYSTEM_OR_FOLDER_NAME].

Requester:       [REQUESTER_NAME], [JOB_TITLE], [DEPARTMENT]
Access needed:   [READ / WRITE / ADMIN] on [RESOURCE_PATH]
Business reason: [REASON_GIVEN_BY_REQUESTER]
Duration:        [PERMANENT / UNTIL_DATE]
Ticket:          [TICKET_ID]

Please reply to this email with "Approved" or "Rejected". Access will be
granted within [SLA_HOURS] business hours of your approval and reviewed
again at the next quarterly access review.

Kind regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
IT Service Desk
```

### 4.5 Resolution confirmation — Xác nhận đã xử lý xong

```text
Subject: [RESOLVED] [TICKET_ID] — [SHORT_ISSUE_TITLE]

Hi [USER_NAME],

Good news — the issue you reported has been resolved.

Issue:       [SHORT_DESCRIPTION]
Cause:       [ROOT_CAUSE_IN_PLAIN_LANGUAGE]
Fix applied: [WHAT_WE_DID]
Resolved at: [RESOLUTION_TIME]

To prevent this happening again, we have [PREVENTIVE_ACTION_OR "added a
knowledge base article for our team"].

Could you please confirm that everything is working on your side? If we
do not hear from you by [AUTO_CLOSE_DATE], the ticket will close
automatically. You can reopen it any time by replying to this email.

Thank you for reporting it — it helps us improve the service.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
IT Service Desk
```

## 5. Bài tập thực hành (Practice Exercises)

### A. Điền từ vào chỗ trống (10 câu)

Từ cho sẵn: *escalate, workaround, lockout, credentials, SLA, replicate, triage, spooler, provision, root cause*

1. Three failed logins triggered an account ____________.
2. I couldn't ____________ the error on my own machine, so I asked for a screen recording.
3. Our ____________ for high-priority tickets is four business hours.
4. Please never share your ____________ with anyone, including IT staff.
5. As a temporary ____________, use the web version of the app.
6. If I can't fix it within 30 minutes, I'll ____________ it to the network team.
7. Restarting the print ____________ usually clears stuck jobs.
8. We need to find the ____________, not just clear the queue every week.
9. HR asked us to ____________ a new mailbox for the engineer starting Monday.
10. Every morning I ____________ the queue and sort tickets by impact.

### B. Matching thuật ngữ ↔ định nghĩa (10 cặp)

| # | Term | | Letter | Definition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | incident | | A | Software that lets Windows communicate with a device |
| 2 | service request | | B | A secure tunnel into the company network from outside |
| 3 | knowledge base | | C | An unplanned interruption to a service |
| 4 | asset tag | | D | Removing all accounts and access when someone leaves |
| 5 | VPN | | E | A standard user request, not a failure |
| 6 | driver | | F | The internal library of fix articles |
| 7 | MFA | | G | Time during which a service is unavailable |
| 8 | deprovision | | H | The ID label used to track a device |
| 9 | downtime | | I | What a user is allowed to do in a system |
| 10 | permissions | | J | A second login step, usually a code or app approval |

### C. Viết lại câu dùng từ cho trước (5 câu)

1. "Restart your computer." → *(lịch sự hơn, dùng* **Could you please** *)*
2. "I don't know why it broke." → *(chuyên nghiệp hơn, dùng* **root cause** *)*
3. "You must wait, we are busy." → *(lịch sự hơn, dùng* **I'm afraid / as soon as** *)*
4. "The system is down. Use the website." → *(dùng* **workaround** *)*
5. "This is not my job." → *(chuyên nghiệp hơn, dùng* **escalate** *)*

### D. Role-play

**Tình huống:** Bạn là IT Helpdesk. Một quản lý người nước ngoài gọi điện, giọng rất bực: máy chiếu trong phòng họp không kết nối được với laptop của ông ấy, và khách hàng đang ngồi chờ trong phòng.
Bạn có **5 phút**. Hãy thực hành: (a) trấn an mà không hứa hão, (b) hỏi đúng 3 câu chẩn đoán, (c) đưa ra một **workaround** ngay lập tức, (d) chốt một cam kết cụ thể về thời gian.
Đổi vai và làm lại — lần hai, người dùng không biết cổng HDMI là gì.
